### Training

Complete the "Working with the IACUC (For Ag and Wildlife)" AALAS course available at: http://www.aalaslearninglibrary.org. 

If you already have an AALAS account, log on and click on the "Libraries" tab at the top of the page. Click on "Animal Care and Use Courses", then select "UF Researchers-IACUC Training Modules" and choose the "Working with the IACUC (For Ag and Wildlife)" course.
 
If you don't already have an AALAS account click "Enroll Now" then select "Myself". Select "Yes I have an Access Code". Enter "ufiacuc" as the code. Create a username and password. Complete all fields required and click on "continue". You will get a notice "Welcome Enrollment Completed". Then hit "continue" again. Click on the "Libraries" tab at the top of the page and click on the Animal Care and Use courses, then the University of Florida Tracks.
